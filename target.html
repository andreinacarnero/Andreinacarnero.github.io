Target Areas

Scorable strikes (yuko datotsu)

There are six prerequisites for scorable strikes:

  1. Strike or stab
  2. Use the proper shinai striking portion (datotsubu)
  3. at the scorable target area (datotsu bui) of the opponent
  4. with great determination,
  5. correct posture,
  6. and zanshin (continuation of awareness after the attack)
